<section class="page-section">
  <h1>Research</h1>
  <p class="lead">
    My research spans coral reef ecosystems, freshwater biodiversity, environmental DNA, bioacoustics,
    and interdisciplinary socio-ecological studies.
  </p>
</section>

<section class="page-section project-list">
  <div class="card">
    <h2>Coral reef bleaching and environmental stressors</h2>
    <p class="project-meta">Coral reef ecology / long-term monitoring / multistressor analysis</p>
    <p>
      I work on coral bleaching dynamics using multi-year monitoring data, with particular interest in how
      local environmental drivers interact with large-scale thermal stress to shape bleaching responses.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h2>eDNA metabarcoding of river fish communities</h2>
    <p class="project-meta">Freshwater ecology / biodiversity / temporal beta diversity</p>
    <p>
      I study fish community structure along river continua using eDNA metabarcoding, with attention to
      spatial gradients, seasonal dynamics, and the balance between species replacement and nestedness.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h2>Bioacoustic monitoring in agricultural landscapes</h2>
    <p class="project-meta">Amphibians / birds / passive acoustic monitoring</p>
    <p>
      Using long-duration audio recordings, I investigate how agricultural land use influences the occurrence
      and diversity of amphibians and birds in subtropical island landscapes.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h2>Socio-ecological systems and environmental governance</h2>
    <p class="project-meta">Interdisciplinary research / wicked problems / sustainability</p>
    <p>
      I am also interested in how ecological knowledge, local governance, and public awareness interact in the
      management of coastal and aquatic environments.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>
